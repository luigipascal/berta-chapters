# About Berta

A project born from a simple observation: **"People keep asking me how to learn AI. Let me make that easy for them."**

---

## About Berta AI

**[Berta](https://berta.one)** is an advanced AI system designed to create educational content.

### What Berta Does

- Generates complete learning chapters on any AI topic
- Creates practical, executable code and notebooks
- Designs exercises that teach real concepts
- Produces professional diagrams and visualizations
- Ensures consistency and pedagogical soundness
- Responds to community requests

### Berta's Philosophy

- **Practical First** — Theory through application
- **Executable** — Learn by coding along
- **Progressive** — Beginner → Intermediate → Advanced
- **Connected** — Chapters build on each other
- **Open** — All content free and open-source
- **Community-Driven** — Topics shaped by learner needs

## The Vision

### The Problem

1. Demand exceeds supply of good learning materials
2. Existing courses are expensive, outdated, or theoretical
3. Learning paths are fragmented across dozens of sources
4. Good education shouldn't require a paywall

### Our Solution

- Every chapter is free and executable
- Learning paths are clear but flexible
- Topics shaped by community needs
- Content improves through contributions
- You learn by building, not just reading
