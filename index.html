    How This Works¶

    Berta Chapters offers two streams of content:

    A structured, 25-chapter journey from Python basics to advanced AI specializations. Start with Chapter 1 and progress through Foundation, Practitioner, and Advanced tracks.

    Custom chapters generated on-demand based on what you need to learn. Request a chapter on any AI topic.
